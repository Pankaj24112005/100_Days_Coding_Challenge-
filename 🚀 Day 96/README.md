A retail company wanted to understand customer shopping behavior to improve:

Customer engagement
Product strategy
Marketing decisions
Sales performance

The analysis focused on factors like discounts, subscriptions, ratings, age groups, and shopping preferences.

Clothing category generated the highest revenue
Young adults contributed the most revenue
Some products heavily depended on discounts for sales
